Planet Plastic

This poster was made to increase awareness of the amount of plastic that is being wasted by humans. It was inspired by a BBC article which referred to Earth as becoming a plastic planet. This in turn inspired me to come up with the phrase "Planet Plastic" and create the visuals which show our planet's name "Planet Earth" crossed out and replaced with "Planet Plastic" to emphasize how much pollution there is.

As the statistic at the bottom states, most of our plastic ends up in the environment. This plastic harms the plants and animals, and eventually will come back into our own bodies as we eat meats that come from animals that have ingested plastic and microplastics. Plastic has even been found at the bottom of the Mariana Trench, the deepest part of the ocean. No place is safe from our harmful pollution and our planet will slowly die unless we act fast to change our habits as a species.
